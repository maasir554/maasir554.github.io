Who are we ?

We are one of the largest hotel chain operating around the globe. We are also known as The Sanskriti Hotel Foundation. we have over 150+ hotels at present. Our staf consist of several people of different cultures and tradition (which is called 'Sanskriti' in Hindi language.) Since the very starting, our team had a great bondiing, which in tun led to the rapid growh of our buisness around the world. Our customers , from the starting are very satisfied by our services and so are we, from the diversity of the customers. Sanskriti Hotels are made to offer premium services and cmfort that everyone demands. We are known for the Luxury we provide to our customers.

Why Sanskriti Hotels?

Our Uniqueness is the answer to this question. We are different from other hotel chains because of the Indian traditional touch in our work culture. Whether you are in any of our branch accross the globe, Indian serving is offered as an option alternatevie to modern hotel service.

Our buildings' achetecture somewhat resembles the traditional Indian Forts, and Mehals. Customers say that they feel to have entered a completely different world, where everything feels Royal.

By paying for our services, you also contribute towards the welfare of the society.

2% of our profit goes to The Sanskriti Welfare Organization, which is an NGO under The Sanskriti Hotels Foundation. This NGO have helped millions poor people to live happily. several poor children got admissions, in reputed Schools without paying the fees, because of this NGO.

Our vision

We are always working to provide comfort to the cusomers, and deliver the best quality of service. We belive that revenue is secondary, but primary focus is customer satisfaction. and we are working on it every day.

The Theme of our Hotels is the Rich Indian Culture. We truly want to make India the 'Golden Bird' again, which it used to be in the good old days.

We belive in the unity of society, we promote all the cultures and traditions, in a positive way.

How we started ?

We started since India got it's independence. Sanskriti Hotels was started from a guest house in Mumbai. A multicultural team founded this hotel chain. In 1947, the first branch was established named as Sanskriti Guest House, which is present day Indian Royal Palace, Mumbai. it had 15 people working in the staff. The building had a small area of 5000 sq. ft. and only two floors. which slowly expanded, by purchasing nesarby buildinngs, and at present it is having an area of 30,000 sq. ft with six floors . The goal was simple. we had to provide luxury to custumers, and this is what we did from the very begining.
